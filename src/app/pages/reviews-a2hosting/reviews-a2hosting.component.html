A2 Hosting FAQs

How do I cancel my A2 Hosting account?

You can cancel your service with A2 Hosting through your online account. After 90 days, you will not be eligible for a refund on their one-year and three-year plans.

Is A2 Hosting Secure?

A2’s hosting plans come with features including SSL certificates, DDoS protection, and regular security updates to its server hardware. The company goes to great lengths to protect its customer’s hosted content, and you can feel confident about using A2’s services if security is a priority to you.

What is the difference between WordPress hosting and web hosting?

WordPress hosting is specifically meant for websites using WordPress, a popular option among people building their first website to seasoned developers. On the other hand, web hosting offers more flexibility around how you build and design your site. Both plans cost the same on A2 Hosting.

Features and Benefits

Every A2 Hosting plan comes with a free cPanel control panel and SSL certificate. You’ll also always have the option of free site migration, which is pretty generous considering some providers charge over $100 for that service! A2 Hosting also offers eco-friendly “green hosting” solutions, and special discounts for non-profit businesses.
As with all providers, other features vary according to hosting type and pricing plan. A2 Hosting gets a score of 4.3/5 in our research for features, which puts it in the middle of the pack – you’ll definitely have what you need, but you may need to pay a little extra to take your site to the next level.
All of A2 Hosting’s shared and WordPress plans include unlimited SSD storage and transfer, as well as a cPanel control panel. These are pretty standard inclusions across the industry. As you move up the pricing tier of the shared and WordPress plans, you’ll also get unlimited email accounts and automatic site backups.
A2 Hosting’s three VPS plans and four dedicated plans offer a mix-and-match of root access, cPanel control panel, and full HostGuard management.

1. Security

In addition to the free SSL certificate included in every plan, A2 Hosting has created its own Perpetual Security initiative, which goes above and beyond to keep your site safe. As part of the initiative, your account comes with free HackScan protection, working 24/7 to prevent attacks on your site. A2 Hosting Perpetual Security includes a whole host (get it?) of other measures, from the virus scanning you’d expect to more exceptional stuff
While doing this A2 Hosting review, I quickly understood that security is A2 Hosting top priority. Many customer reviews showed that their security methods are top-notch. The hosting provider provides free Let’s Encrypt SSL encryption and protection against DDoS and Brute Force attacks. The Dual Hosting Firewall will block unauthorized users, and the Server Hardening strengthens network settings and tightens access control to increase the server’s security. The hosting provider also allows you to set up a 2-factor authentication workflow to access your account, making the login process extra secure.
Suppose you’re willing to pay some extra money. In that case, you can get the Sucuri Website Monitoring, which will enable you to receive scans and alerts, website application firewall protection, and prevention of hackers.

2. Regular Backups

The hosting provider creates an automatic backup of your site. All their plans offer to keep a copy of the last seven days. There’s also an option for manual, automatic backups, cPanel backups, and MySQL database backups. You’ll also be able to backup your data automatically to Google Drive storage or Amazon S3 cloud storage.

3. Email

f you have a simple website or run an online store, having an email is essential. Each available hosting plan comes with free email and unlimited email addresses that you can connect with your domain. If you purchase any plan, you can get email spam protection, webmail software that features pre-installed Horde, Squirrelmail, RoundCube, Email forwarders, and Auto-responders.

4. Customer Support

A2 Hosting hasn’t won our “Best for Customer Service” title for no reason! This provider gets a perfect 5/5 for customer service. The Guru Crew – as A2’s one-to-one assistants like to be called – are available 24/7 to make sure your site runs smoothly.
You also get a range of support options – A2 Hosting provides online tickets, a searchable knowledge base, global phone lines, and a live chat feature, where you can hear directly from the Guru Crew.
This is all seems great at first glance, but we wanted to know how good A2 Hosting’s support really was. So we decided to put it to the test ourselves! Our team tried A2 Hosting’s email, live chat, and knowledge base and rated each one based on our experiences.
The overall score was pretty surprising: A2 Hosting failed to meet our customer support expectations by 9%!
It was A2 Hosting’s knowledge base that really let it down – it was difficult to find answers quickly using its search feature, and in the end it fell below our expectations by 33%. While it might be okay for simple queries, we don’t recommend A2 Hosting’s knowledge base for complex issues!

5. Website Builder

Every Shared Hosting plan contains a free A2 SiteBuilder that will help you build your website as fast as possible. The website builder has incredible features: template selector and customizer, an easy-to-use text editor, contact and sign-up forms, blogs, customized images, and add picture gallery. This website builder is an easy and quick way to get your website up and running.
