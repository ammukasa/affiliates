Features and Ease of Use

HostArmada uses KVM virtualization and guarantees 99.9% uptime with its cloud infrastructure and Redundant Storage replication. This setup makes it easy to hot-swap hardware (CPU units, RAM, and SSD storage) and reduces the chance of the entire system going down.
HostArmada partners with reputable brands for all its plans, including cPanel, CloudFlare, CloudLinux OS (for faster loading speeds from different locations), LiteSpeed, JetApps, Intel, SpamExperts (for email protection), and Imunify360 (to protect servers from hackers and malware).
This host offers three managed cloud shared hosting plans – Start Dock, Web Wrap, and Speed Reaper. Some of the key features you can look forward to include:

With HostArmada shared hosting, you can host from one to unlimited websites. You also get between 15 GB to 40 GB cloud SSD storage space. Cloud SSD drives perform a lot faster than traditional disk drives, hence, boosting your website’s overall speed and performance.
All plans come with unlimited email accounts, unlimited databases, unlimited FTP accounts, and support for multiple PHP versions.
cPanelis the industry-standard control panel, thanks to its extremely user-friendly interface and high functionality. It’s great if you are a beginner or don’t have the time to manage website tasks manually.

Security: is HostArmada secure?

HostArmada has the full stack security solutions in place. It has you covered with measures like SSL certificates, daily backups, DDoS protection, malware scanning, and live server monitoring.
Here’s a brief look:
  • SSL certificates: there’s free SSL from Sectigo for all the domains hosted on it. This is a pretty standard offering and will help to enable an encrypted connection between your site and your visitors.
  • Backups: HostArmada offers between 7 and 21 daily backups – depending on your plan. Although a vital feature, it’s common to only see weekly backups for shared hosting plans – especially budget ones. So, HostArmada is definitely ahead of its competition in this regard.
  • Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) protection: DDoS is an attack where traffic from various compromised systems floods your site to disrupt it. This attack can be devastating so it’s great that HostArmada is armed with server-side protection that includes ModSecurity integration, per-IP throttling, SSL renegotiation protection service, and reCaptcha.
  • Firewalls: includes web application firewall (WAF) that analyzes, monitors, and blocks HTTP traffic at the application layer to prevent XSS attack or SQL injection. This is always a great security measure to minimize your web server’s vulnerability.
  • Malware scanning: HostArmada performs automated security scans for exploits and code-injected malware. You have the option to initiate a security scan via cPanel, too.
Overall, I’m impressed with HostArmada’s sweeping security measures. The features are comprehensive and considerably more than what’s offered by its competitors.
HostArmada offers a variety of security tools for its users. They have taken several security measures to ensure the safety of your websites. These include firewall protection, malware protection, Brute-Force protection and more reliable security features.
